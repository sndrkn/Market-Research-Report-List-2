<p><p>Polyphthalamide (PPA) is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic. It is available in two types: amorphous PPA and semi-crystalline PPA. The amorphous PPA market refers to the demand and supply of PPA with a non-crystalline structure. Amorphous PPA is often used in applications requiring excellent dimensional stability, chemical resistance, and high-temperature capabilities. On the other hand, the semi-crystalline PPA market pertains to PPA with a partially ordered, crystalline structure. Semi-crystalline PPA offers improved mechanical strength, higher impact resistance, and enhanced processability, making it suitable for various demanding applications.</p></p>
