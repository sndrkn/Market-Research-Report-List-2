<p><p>The parchment paper market encompasses two main types: non-stick parchment paper and specialty parchment paper. Non-stick parchment paper is widely used in baking and cooking due to its non-adhesive surface, preventing food from sticking. It provides easy release and simplifies clean-up. On the other hand, specialty parchment paper is designed for specific applications. It may have features like grease resistance, higher heat resistance, or be specifically designed for microwave use.
