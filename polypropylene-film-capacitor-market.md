<p><p>Polypropylene film capacitors are categorized into two main types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metallized polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors are composed of a plain polypropylene film dielectric that offers stable electrical properties and high insulation resistance. They are widely used in electronic circuits for power factor correction, energy storage, and coupling applications. On the other hand, metallized polypropylene film capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the plain polypropylene film, which improves self-healing properties and increases capacitance density. They are commonly used in high-frequency filtering and bypass applications in various electronic devices.</p></p>
