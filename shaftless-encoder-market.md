<p><p>The shaftless encoder market includes three main types: magnetic encoders, capacitive encoders, and optical encoders. Magnetic encoders use magnetism to detect position, providing high accuracy and reliability. Capacitive encoders operate by measuring changes in capacitance, offering robustness in challenging environments. Optical encoders use light and sensors to determine position, delivering precise measurements and high resolution. These encoder types find applications in various industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automotive, where accurate position sensing is crucial for equipment control and automation.</p></p>
