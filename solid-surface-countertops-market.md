<p><strong>The Solid Surface Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid surface countertops are available in different types such as press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves using heat and pressure to shape solid surface materials into countertops. This method ensures a uniform thickness and is ideal for creating intricate patterns. On the other hand, casting molding involves pouring liquid solid surface material into molds and allowing it to harden. This technique allows for more flexibility in shapes and designs, making it popular for customized countertops. Both press molding and casting molding offer a wide range of options for consumers in the solid surface countertop market.</p></p>
