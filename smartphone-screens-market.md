<p><p>There are several types of smartphone screens available in the market. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) screens use liquid crystals to produce images and offer good brightness and color accuracy. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, resulting in deeper blacks and vibrant colors. AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens are a type of OLED display where each pixel has a separate transistor, providing faster response times. Other types of smartphone screens include IPS (In-Plane Switching) LCD and TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) LCD, which offer their own benefits and are used in various devices.</p></p>
