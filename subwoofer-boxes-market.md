<p><p>The subwoofer boxes market consists of two main types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers are self-contained units that have built-in amplifiers to power the subwoofer. They are often compact and don't require external amplifiers, making them easy to set up. Passive subwoofers, on the other hand, need to be powered by an external amplifier. They are typically larger and provide more powerful bass reproduction.
