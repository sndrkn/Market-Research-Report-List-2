<p><ul><li>Plugins</li><li>Patches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic feedthrough capacitors in the market are available in two main types: plugins and patches. Plugin capacitors are designed to be inserted into a hole and secured in place using mechanical means, such as mounting nuts or screws. On the other hand, patch capacitors are surface-mounted on the PCB using soldering techniques. Both types serve the same purpose of transferring electrical signals or power through a barrier, but their installation methods and form factors differ, providing flexibility for different application requirements.</p></p>
