<p><p>Commercial Ethernet switches are networking devices used to connect devices on a local area network (LAN). There are three types of switches in the market. Fixed Managed switches offer advanced features like VLANs, security, and QoS, and are suitable for medium to large businesses. Fixed Unmanaged switches are basic plug-and-play devices without any management capabilities, making them ideal for small businesses or home setups. Modular switches provide scalability and flexibility as they allow expansion by adding modules for additional ports or features, catering to large enterprises with evolving network requirements.</p></p>
