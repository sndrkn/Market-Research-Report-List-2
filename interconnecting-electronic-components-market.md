<p><p>Interconnecting Electronic Components market consists of various types such as Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs), Connectors/Sockets, Switches, Relays, and Others. Connectors/Sockets provide a means for connecting various electronic devices or components together. Switches allow the control of electrical circuits, enabling the flow or interruption of electric current. Relays are electromechanical switches that control high-power circuits with low-power control signals. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses various interconnecting components like cables, wires, and terminals used in electronic systems.</p></p>
