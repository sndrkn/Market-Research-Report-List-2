<p><p>Back-contact heterojunction solar cells is a type of solar cell technology that has gained popularity in the market. There are three main types: IBC (interdigitated back contact) solar cells, EWT (Emitter Wrap Through) solar cells, and MWT (Metal Wrap Through) solar cells. IBC solar cells have contacts on the back surface, minimizing shading and improving performance. EWT solar cells have contacts on the front and back of the cell, allowing for higher efficiency and flexibility. MWT solar cells have metal connectors on the rear surface, increasing the front surface available for absorption and thus improving performance.</p></p>
