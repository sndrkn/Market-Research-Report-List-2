<p><p>The Access Control Fingerprint Chip market includes two main types: the Capacitive Fingerprint Module and the Optical Fingerprint Module. The Capacitive Fingerprint Module utilizes electrical fields to capture the fingerprint's unique pattern by measuring changes in capacitance. On the other hand, the Optical Fingerprint Module uses light to capture the fingerprint image and analyze its details. Both technologies offer reliable and secure access control solutions, ensuring accurate identification and authentication for various applications, including smartphones, security systems, and financial transactions.</p></p>
