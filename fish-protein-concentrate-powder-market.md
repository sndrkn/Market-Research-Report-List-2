<p><ul><li>Primary Concentration</li><li>High Concentration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fish Protein Concentrate Powder market can be categorized into two types: Primary Concentration and High Concentration. Primary Concentration refers to the initial stage of processing where fish meat is first converted into a water-soluble paste via hydrolysis. This paste contains a moderate protein concentration. On the other hand, High Concentration involves further processing of the primary concentrate to obtain a product with a higher protein content. This is achieved by removing excess water through various techniques such as filtration, centrifugation, or drying methods.</p></p>
