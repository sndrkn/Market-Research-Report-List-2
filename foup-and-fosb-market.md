<p><p>FOUP and FOSB are both market types for wafer transport and storage in the semiconductor industry. FOUP refers to Front Opening Unified Pod, which is a standardized and environmentally controlled cassette used to store and transport wafers. FOSB, on the other hand, stands for Front Opening Shipping Box, which is a larger container used to transport multiple FOUPs. While FOUPs are primarily used within the fabrication facilities, FOSBs play a crucial role in shipping wafers between different sites, ensuring the safe transportation and protection of sensitive semiconductor devices.</p></p>
