<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Pigments refer to coloring agents used in cosmetics and personal care products. Water-soluble pigments are pigments that can easily dissolve in water, making them suitable for water-based cosmetics like foundations and moisturizers. On the other hand, oil-soluble pigments do not dissolve in water but can mix with oils, making them ideal for oil-based cosmetics such as lipsticks and mascara.
