<p><p>Fused Cast AZS (alumina-zirconia-silica) refractories are highly durable materials used in high-temperature applications, primarily in the glass and steel industries. These refractories are produced by melting a mixture of alumina, zirconia, and silica in an electric furnace and then cooling it to form a solid block. The unique composition of AZS refractories gives them excellent resistance to corrosion, thermal shock, and abrasion, making them ideal for lining furnaces, kilns, and other high-temperature equipment.</p><p>The future outlook for the Fused Cast AZS Refractories Market is positive, with a projected CAGR of 11.9% during the forecasted period.
