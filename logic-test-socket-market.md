<p><p>Logic Test Socket is a market that comprises various types such as PoP (Package-on-Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead), FBGA (Fine Pitch Ball Grid Array), SOP (Small Outline Package), and Others. PoP sockets enable testing of stacked chips, while QFN sockets are used for testing small-sized and low-profile packages. FBGA sockets cater to high-density ball grid array packages and SOP sockets are suitable for testing small-sized devices. The "Others" category may include various socket types not specifically mentioned. Overall, the Logic Test Socket market encompasses a range of socket options to meet different package requirements.</p></p>
