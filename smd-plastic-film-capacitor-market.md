<p><p>The SMD Plastic Film Capacitor market is classified into three types: PEN, PET, and PPS. </p><p>PEN (Polyethylene Napthalate) capacitors offer high capacitance stability and excellent self-healing properties, making them suitable for applications requiring long-term reliability. </p><p>PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) capacitors provide high insulation resistance and low dielectric absorption, making them ideal for applications requiring high-frequency performance. </p><p>PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) capacitors offer high temperature resistance and superior stability, making them suitable for demanding applications in harsh environments. Overall, each type of capacitor caters to specific requirements, providing reliable electrical performance in various applications.</p></p>
