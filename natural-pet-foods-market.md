<p><p>The natural pet food market consists of two main types: dry food and wet food. Dry food is made by dehydrating the ingredients, resulting in a convenient and shelf-stable product. It is often preferred for its affordability and longer shelf life. Wet food, on the other hand, is made with higher moisture content, providing hydration to pets. It is usually sold in cans or pouches and is considered more palatable for picky eaters. Both dry and wet pet food options cater to different pet preferences and nutritional needs.</p></p>
