<p><strong>The DUV Lithography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Submerged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) Lithography machines are used in semiconductor manufacturing to transfer circuit patterns onto silicon wafers. These machines come in two market types: dry and submerged. In dry lithography, the lens is exposed to air, while in submerged lithography, the lens is submerged in a liquid medium. Dry lithography machines are more common due to lower complexity and cost. Submerged lithography machines offer improved resolution and depth of focus but are less widely adopted. Both types play a crucial role in the production of advanced microchips, catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
