<p><p>Multi-purpose diodes for ESD protection are electronic devices used to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge. The TVS diodes, or transient voltage suppressor diodes, are designed to quickly clamp and absorb high voltage transients. Switching diodes allow current flow in one direction only and are commonly used for signal switching applications. Rectifier diodes permit current flow in one direction and are widely used in power supplies and AC-to-DC converters. The "others" category includes various diodes such as varactor diodes, Zener diodes, and Schottky diodes, each offering unique properties for ESD protection in specific applications.</p></p>
