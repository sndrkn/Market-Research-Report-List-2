Moreover, the cost-effectiveness of dairy blends compared to natural butter is attracting manufacturers in the food industry to opt for these blends.</p><p>In terms of market growth analysis, the dairy blends market is witnessing significant expansion due to the growing popularity of bakery and confectionery products.
