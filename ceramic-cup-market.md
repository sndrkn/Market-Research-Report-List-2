<p><p>The ceramic cup market is categorized based on temperature resistance levels. Low-temperature ceramic cups can withstand temperatures between 700 to 900°C, making them suitable for everyday use. Medium-temperature ceramic cups are capable of handling temperatures from 1000 to 1200°C, making them ideal for microwave or oven use. High-temperature ceramic cups can endure temperatures equal to or exceeding 1200°C, making them suitable for industrial applications like metal casting or kiln firing. These temperature resistance levels determine the specific applications and durability of ceramic cups in various market segments.</p></p>
