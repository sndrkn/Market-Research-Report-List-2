<p><p>The Medical Modified Polypropylene Market can be divided into two types based on the modifications made. Physical modification involves changing the physical properties of polypropylene without altering its chemical structure. This can be achieved by methods like stretching or blending with other materials. On the other hand, chemical modification involves altering the chemical structure of polypropylene to enhance its properties. This is typically done through processes like grafting or copolymerization. Both physical and chemical modifications play a crucial role in improving the performance and suitability of polypropylene for medical applications.</p></p>
