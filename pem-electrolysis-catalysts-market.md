<p><p>PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) Electrolysis Catalysts refer to catalysts used in electrolysis cells that operate with a proton exchange membrane as the electrolyte. The market for PEM Electrolysis Catalysts can be further categorized into two types: Oxygen Evolution Reaction (OER) Catalysts and Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER) Catalysts. OER Catalysts facilitate the production of oxygen gas from water during the electrolysis process, while HER Catalysts assist in the generation of hydrogen gas. Both catalyst types are crucial for efficient and sustainable electrolysis-based hydrogen production, an essential component of the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
