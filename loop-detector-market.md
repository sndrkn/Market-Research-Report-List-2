<p><p>A loop detector is an electronic device used in traffic control systems to detect the presence of vehicles at intersections or on roadways. It consists of a loop of wire buried under the road surface that emits an electromagnetic field. When a vehicle passes over the loop, it disrupts the field, signaling the control system to change traffic signals or activate other road functions.</p><p>The future outlook of the loop detector market is promising, with projected growth at a CAGR of 4.80% during the forecasted period. The increasing demand for efficient traffic management systems, along with the need for real-time traffic data analysis, is driving the market growth.</p><p>Currently, there is a growing adoption of loop detectors in smart cities and intelligent transportation systems.
