<p><strong>The Acoustic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acoustic Filter market consists of two major types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW). SAW filters employ a piezoelectric substrate to generate acoustic waves, which are then used to transmit or filter signals. BAW filters, on the other hand, use a layered structure to generate and propagate acoustic waves, enabling efficient signal filtering. Both SAW and BAW filters find applications in various industries, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive, due to their ability to enhance signal quality and reduce interference.</p></p>
