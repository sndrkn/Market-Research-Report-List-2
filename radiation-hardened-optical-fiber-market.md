<p><p>Radiation Hardened Optical Fiber is divided into two primary types in the market: Single Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Single Mode Fiber offers a narrow core that allows only one pathway for light transmission, enabling high-speed communication over long distances. It is commonly used in telecommunications and long-haul communication systems. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber has a larger core that allows multiple pathways for light transmission. It is more suitable for shorter distance communication such as within buildings or local area networks. These fiber types differ in their core size and transmission capabilities, catering to various communication needs.</p></p>
