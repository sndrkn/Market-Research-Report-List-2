<p><p>Silica cat litter market comprises two main types: bentonite cat litter and crystal cat litter. Bentonite cat litter is made from clay and forms clumps when urine is absorbed, making it easier to clean. Crystal cat litter, on the other hand, is composed of absorbent silica gel crystals that control odor and absorb moisture. Both types offer excellent odor control and low maintenance, but crystal cat litter tends to last longer and requires less frequent changing.</p></p>
