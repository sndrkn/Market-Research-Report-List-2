<p><p>Black foam glass is a unique material used in various applications. It is available in two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass consists of numerous sealed cells, which offer excellent insulation properties and high compressive strength. It is mainly used in construction and insulation applications. On the other hand, open cell foam glass contains interconnected cells, providing enhanced sound absorption and lightweight characteristics. It finds applications in soundproofing, filtration, and drainage systems. Both types of foam glass are highly versatile and contribute to sustainability and energy efficiency efforts.</p></p>
