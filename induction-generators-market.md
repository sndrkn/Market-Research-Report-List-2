High voltage induction generators are designed to operate at a higher voltage range and are commonly used in large-scale industrial applications. Medium voltage induction generators are suitable for medium-sized systems that require moderate power output. Low voltage induction generators are typically used in residential or small commercial applications where the power demand is relatively low.
