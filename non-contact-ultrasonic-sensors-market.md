Open type sensors are designed with an open structure, allowing sound waves to propagate freely, enabling long sensing ranges and versatile applications. Sealed type sensors, on the other hand, are enclosed in a protective casing, making them suitable for harsh environments and providing better protection against dust, moisture, and other contaminants.</p></p>
