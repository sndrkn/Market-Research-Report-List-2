<p><p>The commercial food display cabinets market consists of two main types: refrigerated food display cabinets and heated food display cabinets. Refrigerated food display cabinets are designed to keep perishable items such as beverages, salads, and desserts cool and fresh. They typically have glass doors to allow customers to view the items while protecting them from outside contaminants. Heated food display cabinets, on the other hand, are used to keep cooked food warm, such as soups, pizzas, and pastries. They help maintain the optimal temperature and visibility of the food to attract customers.</p></p>
