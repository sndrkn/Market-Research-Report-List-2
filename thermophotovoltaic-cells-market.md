<p><p>Thermophotovoltaic (TPV) cells are a type of energy conversion technology that utilizes thermal radiation to generate electricity. The market for TPV cells can be categorized into two types: epitaxial method cells and non-epitaxial method cells. Epitaxial method cells involve the growth of a thin semiconductor layer on a suitable substrate. On the other hand, non-epitaxial method cells are manufactured using materials that are directly compatible with the desired spectral range. Both types of cells aim to efficiently convert thermal radiation into electricity but differ in their manufacturing techniques.</p></p>
