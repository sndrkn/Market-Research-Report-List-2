<p><strong>The FLNG Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LNG FPSO</li><li>FSRU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FLNG market consists of two main types: LNG FPSO and FSRU. LNG FPSO refers to Floating Production Storage and Offloading vessels that are specifically designed to produce, store, and offload liquefied natural gas (LNG) from offshore gas fields. They are capable of liquefaction and storage of LNG onboard. FSRU, on the other hand, stands for Floating Storage and Regasification Units, which are utilized for the storage and regasification of LNG. FSRUs receive LNG from carriers, store it onboard, and then convert it back into natural gas for distribution.</p></p>
