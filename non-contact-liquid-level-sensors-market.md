<p><strong>The Non-contact Liquid Level Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Liquid Level Sensors</li><li>Radar Liquid Level Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact liquid level sensors include ultrasonic liquid level sensors and radar liquid level sensors. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure the level of liquid in a container by transmitting signals and calculating the time they take to bounce back. Radar sensors, on the other hand, use electromagnetic waves to determine the liquid level by measuring the time it takes for the waves to reflect back from the liquid surface. These sensors offer accurate and reliable measurements without the need for physical contact with the liquid, making them suitable for various industries and applications.</p></p>
