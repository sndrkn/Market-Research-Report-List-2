<p><p>Sound-proof booths, also known as acoustic booths or isolation booths, are enclosed structures that are designed to minimize the transmission of sound from inside to outside and vice versa. The increasing demand for noise reduction solutions in various industries, including entertainment, healthcare, and manufacturing, is expected to drive the market growth.
