<p><p>Electric milk frothers are a popular device used to create frothy milk for coffee or other beverages. They come in two main types: handheld and desktop. Handheld frothers are portable and operate by immersing a whisk-like attachment into a container of milk, creating foam by rotating rapidly. Desktop frothers, on the other hand, are larger and stationary devices that feature a container where milk is placed, and use a motor to whisk the milk to create froth. Both types provide convenient and efficient frothing options for coffee lovers.</p></p>
