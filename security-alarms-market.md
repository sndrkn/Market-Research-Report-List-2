<p><p>The security alarms market can be categorized into three main types: wired, wireless, and hybrid systems. Wired alarms require physical wiring for installation and connection to a central monitoring system. Wireless alarms, on the other hand, use radio frequency signals to communicate between sensors and the central monitoring unit, eliminating the need for wiring. Hybrid systems combine both wired and wireless components, offering a flexible and customizable security solution. Each of these types has its advantages and considerations, allowing consumers to choose the system that best fits their specific needs.</p></p>
