<p><p>Solar Radiation Smart Sensors are devices used to measure and monitor solar radiation. The market types include Silicon Cell Type and Thermopile Type. Silicon Cell Type sensors use silicon semi-conductors to convert sunlight into electricity, measuring the voltage produced to determine radiation levels. Thermopile Type sensors utilize thermocouples to measure the temperature difference caused by solar radiation, providing a voltage output that corresponds to the radiation levels. Both types offer accurate and reliable measurements of solar radiation, catering to different requirements and applications in various industries.</p></p>
