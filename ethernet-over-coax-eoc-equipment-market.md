<p><p>Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment refers to the technology that allows the transmission of Ethernet signals over existing coaxial cable infrastructure. There are two main types of EoC equipment in the market. Passive EoC equipment uses the existing coaxial cables solely for data transmission without any signal amplification or enhancement, making it a cost-effective solution for short-distance applications. On the other hand, active EoC equipment enhances the Ethernet signal over longer distances by using advanced technologies like signal amplification, equalization, and filtering, which makes it suitable for longer cable runs in larger networks.</p></p>
