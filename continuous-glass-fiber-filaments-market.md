<p><p>Continuous glass fiber filaments are long, thin strands of glass that are extensively used in various industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and electronics. These filaments are widely used as reinforcement materials in composite applications, where they enhance the mechanical properties of the end product.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the continuous glass fiber filaments market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period.
